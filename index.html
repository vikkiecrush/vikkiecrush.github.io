01 · About Me

Professional Bio

I am a Senior Data Scientist with a Master of Science in Applied Statistics & Decision Analytics and am currently pursuing a graduate degree in Artificial Intelligence at Indiana Wesleyan University. My experience spans geophysics, retail supply chain, and last-mile e-commerce, where I've worked at the intersection of data, operations, and business strategy to solve complex, real-world problems.

Having also co-managed a last-mile delivery business, I understand that every metric ultimately represents people — customers, associates, drivers, and operators making decisions every day. That perspective shapes how I approach data science. I believe the best solutions begin with understanding the business problem and the people affected by it, then applying statistical rigor, machine learning, and AI to deliver practical, measurable outcomes.

As I continue to grow in my career, my goal is to become an AI leader who helps organizations adopt artificial intelligence responsibly, ethically, and with a clear focus on creating value for both businesses and the people they serve.
